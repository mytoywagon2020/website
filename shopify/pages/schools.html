On collecting, on cataloging

A small cabinet, carefully kept.

Procurement, like a child's specimen cabinet, is a quiet practice of cataloging. Each cork-stoppered bottle holds one thing, carefully labeled, ready when the season asks for it.

We try to bring the same care to the paperwork. A W-9 in its right place. A tax-exempt certificate on file. A PO matched to its quote. Small acts of order, in service of larger ones.

Most school-supply catalogs read like spreadsheets. We are trying to read like a letter.

My Toy Wagon serves schools, libraries, therapy practices, and homeschool co-ops with the same heirloom wooden and felt toys we send to homes — wood, wool, hand-stitched, hand-painted, built to outlast a school year and most of the children who passed through that classroom. This page is the procurement side of the conversation: the paperwork, the payment terms, and the path from interested to ordered.

From interested to ordered, in four steps.

Most school orders move from inquiry to delivery in two to four weeks.

i.

Apply for an Educator account.

Apply at our Educator Program page. Approval is usually same-day, never longer than two business days.

ii.

Build a quote.

Browse the B2B catalog with educator pricing. Save items to a draft, add notes, and submit.

iii.

We invoice.

We review your quote, adjust if needed, and send a formal invoice with PO matching.

iv.

You pay, we ship.

Pay by PO, check, ACH, or card. We ship from our LA warehouse, with tracking emailed the day it leaves.

For your accounts-payable team.

The procurement details most schools and districts need on file.

i.

Payment terms we accept

We work with how schools actually pay.

  • Purchase orders (Net-30 default)
  • Net-60 invoicing for larger orders
  • School and district checks
  • ACH bank transfer
  • Wire transfer (international)
  • Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
ii.

Tax-exempt purchasing

Once you upload your tax-exempt certificate, sales tax is removed from all future invoices.

  • State-issued certificate, or
  • 501(c)(3) determination letter
  • Re-uploaded every 3 years or when state requires

Required for tax-exempt status; otherwise sales tax is added per California regulations.

iii.

Documents on file

Most schools require these from us. All are available on request.

  • W-9 (signed and dated)
  • California seller's permit (CDTFA-issued license number)
  • Resale certificate (signed form for tax-exempt resale purchases)
  • Certificate of insurance
  • Business license
  • Custom vendor forms (we complete and return)

See our vendor profile page for downloads and email-request options.

iv.

Shipping to schools

We ship to schools, classrooms, district warehouses, and loading docks.

  • Shipping is quoted per order, based on size and destination
  • Standard ground shipping for most orders
  • Drop-shipping to multiple classroom addresses available
  • Insurance certificate available for loading-dock deliveries

For district-wide orders shipping to a central warehouse, email us first for a coordinated quote.

Procurement notes

The fine print, plainly stated.

Answers to the questions schools ask after a quote and before a PO.

Stock confirmed on every quote.

Every quote we send confirms current inventory on every line item. If anything is short, we flag it before you submit a PO.

Pricing held for 30 days.

Quoted pricing is honored for 30 calendar days from the quote date. Plenty of time for district approval and PO issuance.

If something goes out of stock.

If a quoted item becomes unavailable between quote and PO, we will substitute with your written approval or remove it from the order.

Ship to multiple classrooms.

Drop-ship to multiple classroom addresses on one PO. We do this regularly. Email for a coordinated quote.

Sole-source letters, on request.

For districts that require a non-competitive-bid justification (Tara Treasures, Bauspiel, and other genuinely unique brands), we write the letter within 2 business days.

Tax-exempt, all 50 states.

We accept tax-exempt certificates from any state. Once on file, sales tax is waived on every future invoice automatically.

Sustainability, on the record.

For green-school questionnaires: Tara Treasures is Fair Trade USA Certified, Bauspiel and Drewart use FSC-certified European hardwood, and most of our line is OEKO-TEX or GOTS certified.

Cancellations after fulfillment.

PO cancelled after pick-and-pack has begun is subject to a 15% restocking fee plus return shipping. Cancellations before fulfillment are free.

Payment terms.

Net-30 is our default. Net-60 available for larger or recurring orders. Net-60 is our maximum; extensions are reviewed case by case, based on need — email accounting with your situation.

References from currently-served schools available on request.
