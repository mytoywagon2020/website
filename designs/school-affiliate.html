Featured maker · Tara Treasures

Designed in Melbourne. Hand-felted in Nepal. Fair Trade Certified.

Tara Treasures is a small Melbourne studio founded by Jag Gurung and Jooli Chan. After the 2015 Nepal earthquake, the two returned to Jag's home country with a vision to create sustainable work for local artisans. They started by selling at Melbourne markets. They were the first toy brand in Australia to gain Fairtrade Certification.

Today, over 350 women artisans in Nepal hand-stitch every piece, working from home or in small community workshops so they can balance work with family. The wool is 100% GOTS-certified New Zealand merino, dyed with azo-free, non-toxic colorants. No glues, no polyester filler, no shortcuts.

Their licensed collections include May Gibbs' Gumnut Babies and Julia Donaldson's The Gruffalo. Their work is endorsed by The Wiggles and CNN Hero of the Year Anuradha Koirala. We carry the range because the craft and the conscience match: a Tara Treasures playmat lives on a child's floor for the better part of a decade, made by hands the same size as their own.

"Every child deserves to play with toys that are safe, beautiful, and fair trade."Jooli Chan, co-founder · Tara Treasures
