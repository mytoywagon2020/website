What you'll get.

Four practical things, designed for the way schools and therapy practices actually order toys.

i.

Quote-and-PO ordering.

Browse our Educator catalog, build a quote, and request a formal invoice. Pay by purchase order, credit card, or check. Itemized PDFs available for your accounting team.

ii.

Net-30 or Net-60 invoicing.

Verified schools and licensed therapists qualify for Net-30 terms. Net-60 available for larger orders or district-wide purchases.

iii.

Replacement parts and repair.

For every wooden set we carry, we keep a small inventory of spare pieces. A misplaced cube or a chipped tile does not mean replacing the whole set.

iv.

One-on-one curation.

Schedule a 30-minute call with our team for help selecting age-appropriate, classroom-tested toys. Free for all Educator account holders.

How it works.

Four steps. Approval is usually same-day, never longer than two business days.

i.

Apply below.

Fill out the form. Most schools and therapists are approved within 24 hours.

ii.

Upload documents.

Tax-exempt certificate, W-9 if relevant, school or practice verification.

iii.

Browse the Educator catalog.

The full My Toy Wagon catalog with exclusive bundles. Save items to a draft, add notes, and submit.

iv.

You pay, we ship.

Pay by PO, check, ACH, or card. We ship most orders from our LA warehouse, with tracking emailed the day it leaves.
