Welcome, and a few words.

If you are reading this, your school or organization is preparing to issue a purchase order to our shop, and we want to make that as straightforward as possible.

My Toy Wagon is a small family-run business in Arcadia, California, online since 2020. We serve schools, libraries, therapy practices, and homeschool co-ops with the same natural wooden and felt toys we send to homes —lt toys we send to homes — wood, wool, hand-stitched, hand-painted, built to last a decade of classroom use.

Our procurement process is intentionally short. Most school orders move from quote to confirmed PO in three to five business days. We complete your district's vendor form within one business day of receipt. We accept tax-exempt certificates from all fifty states. We bill Net-30 by default, with Net-60 available for larger orders.

The pages that follow lay out the full process, payment terms, ordering options, and a list of procurement notes — the questions schools tend to ask after a quote has been issued and before a purchase order is signed.

If your team has a question that is not answered here, please write to us. We answer every procurement inquiry within one business day.

My Toy Wagon Process & payment
How an order moves through our shop

From quote, to PO, to delivery.

01

Educator account

Apply at mytoywagon.com/educator. Verification takes 1–2 business days. Once approved, your account sees educator pricing across the full catalog, plus access to the Educator Portal for quotes and order history.

02

Build a quote

Use the in-portal quote builder, or email [email protected]. Every quote we send confirms current stock on every line, includes itemized educator pricing, and lists shipping (or "shipping calculated at PO"). Quoted pricing is held for 30 calendar days.

03

Submit a purchase order

Reference the quote number on your PO. Email to [email protected]. We confirm receipt within one business day, with PO acknowledgment for your AP records.

04

Fulfillment & invoicing

Pick-and-pack begins within two business days. Most orders ship within five business days; large orders ship within two to three weeks. The invoice is issued at shipment, with terms (Net-30 or Net-60) as agreed at quote.

Payment terms & accepted methods
Default terms
Net-30 from invoice dateAvailable to all verified schools and licensed therapists.
Extended terms
Net-60, available on requestFor larger or recurring orders. Net-60 is our maximum; extensions are reviewed case by case based on need — email [email protected] with your situation.
Accepted payment
School / district check · Purchase order (Net invoicing) · ACH transfer · Major credit cards
Tax-exempt
We accept tax-exempt certificates from all 50 states. Once on file, sales tax is waived on every future invoice automatically.

My Toy Wagon Procurement notes
Answers to the questions schools ask

Procurement notes, plainly stated.

Answers to the questions schools tend to ask after a quote has been issued and before a purchase order has been signed.

Stock confirmed on every quote.

Every quote we send confirms current inventory on every line item. If anything is short, we flag it before you submit a PO.

Pricing held for 30 days.

Quoted pricing is honored for 30 calendar days from quote date. Plenty of time for district approval and PO issuance.

If something goes out of stock.

If a quoted item becomes unavailable between quote and PO, we will substitute with your written approval or remove it from the order.

Vendor forms, one business day.

Send your district's vendor onboarding form to [email protected] and we will return it completed within one business day.

Ship to multiple classrooms.

Drop-ship to multiple classroom addresses on one PO. We do this regularly. Email [email protected] for a coordinated quote.

Sole-source letters, on request.

For districts that require a non-competitive-bid justification (Tara Treasures, Bauspiel, and other genuinely unique brands), we write the letter within two business days.

Sustainability, on the record.

Tara Treasures is Fair Trade USA Certified. Bauspiel and Drewart use FSC-certified European hardwood. Most of our line is OEKO-TEX or GOTS certified.

Cancellations after fulfillment.

PO cancelled after pick-and-pack has begun is subject to a 15% restocking fee plus return shipping. Cancellations before fulfillment are free.

References from served schools.

Available on request. Email [email protected] and we will share institution references appropriate to your size and program type.
