# My Toy Wagon — Brand Guide

> Heirloom toys, curated by hand. A family-owned boutique, online since 2020.

## Positioning

My Toy Wagon is a family-run boutique toy shop in Los Angeles, California, online
since 2020. We curate wooden toys, Waldorf dolls, felt playscapes, and open-ended
play sets, sourced from makers we know by name. Every piece is chosen against a
single criterion: **a toy is worth keeping if it scales across a childhood and
passes to the sibling who comes after.**

We are not a big-box toy store. We are a small, opinionated shelf — quiet,
heirloom, and built to last.

## Brand promise

- **Kept, not outgrown.** Toys meant to be kept, not outgrown.
- **Curated by hand.** Every brand vetted for safety, materials, and a finish
  that survives a sibling.
- **Packed by hand.** Most orders are packed and shipped by hand from our shop
  in Los Angeles.

## Taglines

### Typography

- **Headings & editorial accents:** Cormorant Garamond (serif). Used at large
  sizes, often with italics for leads, captions, and pull-quotes.
- **Body & UI:** Mulish (sans-serif). Weights 400–700.

### Tone of the design

Generous whitespace, hairline rules, small uppercase eyebrows with wide letter-
spacing, 4:3 and 4:5 image ratios, warm cinematic photography. Motion is gentle
and respects reduced-motion preferences. Nothing shouts.

## What we sell

Wooden toys, Waldorf and soft dolls, felt mats & playscapes, sensory and
loose-parts play, kitchen play, wooden animals, ride-ons, woodland homes & fairy
houses, nature play, nursery & first toys, and build & construct sets.

## Values

- **Heirloom quality** — well enough made to pass to a younger sibling.
- **Safety first** — ASTM F963 (US), EN 71 (EU), CPSIA; non-toxic, water-based
  stains; BPA-free; teething-safe finishes.
- **Responsible materials** — FSC-certified European timber; beeswax and organic
  oil finishes; plant-based, compostable where possible.
- **Honesty** — plain language, no jargon. If something is back-ordered or sold
  out, we say so on the card.
