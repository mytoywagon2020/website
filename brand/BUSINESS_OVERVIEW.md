## Our story

My Toy Wagon began with a simple inspiration: our two daughters, Hafsa and Noor.
They are the heartbeat of the shop — the reminders that childhood is fleeting,
and that the toys we choose for it matter. We carefully curate wooden and felt
toys that hold up to real play, scale across ages, and pass quietly to a younger
sibling. Most orders are packed and shipped by hand from our shop in Los Angeles.

## Catalog structure

### Categories
Sensory & discovery · Wooden animals · Kitchen play · Felt mats & playscapes ·
Ride-ons · Woodland homes & fairy houses · Nature play · Nursery & playroom ·

### Curation criterion
A toy earns its shelf space if it **scales across childhood and across a sibling.**

## Customer policies

- **Shipping:** Free U.S. shipping on orders $150 and up (contiguous U.S.).
- **Returns:** Free 30-day returns. We pay return shipping if an item arrived
  damaged, defective, or wrong; a small return-shipping fee applies to
  change-of-mind returns.
- **Package protection:** Route Package Protection offered at checkout (optional).
- **Gifting:** Tell us at checkout it's a gift and we leave the price off the
  packing slip. Digital gift cards are delivered by email with a personal note.
- **Some items ship from partner warehouses** (e.g., select HABA and Candylab
  items ship within the 48 contiguous U.S. only and can't be expedited).
