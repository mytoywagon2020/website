# My Toy Wagon — Voice & Content Guide

## Voice in one line

Warm, literary, and unhurried — a knowledgeable friend who runs a small shop and
writes the way they talk. Editorial, never salesy.

## Principles

1. **Sensory and specific.** Name the material, the weight, the sound, the light.
   "Smooth beechwood worn round as a river stone" beats "high-quality wood."
2. **Developmental, not pushy.** Frame the value as what it opens for the child
   (grip, cause-and-effect, focus), not "buy now."
3. **Heirloom motif.** Return to the throughline: scales across childhood, passes
   to the next sibling, kept for years.
4. **Plain and honest.** No jargon, no hype. Say it plainly. If it's back-ordered
   or sold out, say so.
5. **Quiet confidence.** Short, calm sentences. Em-dashes for rhythm. Let the
   product breathe.

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "A rattle that teaches — every shake earns a sound." | "Best educational toy ever!!!" |
| "Made in Germany to HABA's exacting standards." | "Premium high-quality materials." |
| State the real manufacturer age grade. | Copy unreliable inherited age tags. |
| Use em-dashes, occasional second person. | Exclamation spam, ALL CAPS, emoji. |

## Product description template (PDP)

Use this structure for every product. Keep markup clean (no leftover `<meta>`,
`data-mce-*`, page-builder, or review-widget HTML).

1. **Lead paragraph** — 2–3 sentences: what it is + the play / developmental value,
   in brand voice. No shipping disclaimers here.
2. **Why you'll love it** — 3–5 benefit bullets (bold lead-in + plain explanation).
3. **What's included** — bullet list of contents (sets only).
4. **The details** — specs pulled from data, not prose:
   Materials · Dimensions · Age (manufacturer grade) · Safety/certifications ·
   Country of origin · Care.
5. **Shipping note** — only if a warehouse/ship restriction applies; placed at the
   bottom in small text, never bold at the top.
6. **SEO** — a 150–160 character meta description generated from the lead.

### Age & safety rule
Age comes from the **manufacturer's grade + safety certification regime**
(ASTM F963 / EN 71 / CPSIA per category), verified per product. Do not trust
inherited Shopify tags (~60% accurate). Correct the `custom.age_band` metafield
and `age:*` tags to match the verified grade.

## Blog / editorial

- Long-form, first-person-plural ("we"), shop-voiced.
- Cadence: a new post roughly every other Thursday under "Reading, slowly."
- Lanes: **Field note** (what's arrived / maker introductions),
  **From the maker** (workshop & material stories),
  **Care & storage** (how-to guides).
- Open with a lead and an italic dek. Use section headers, benefit bullets, a
  "Shop the post" block, and a closing pull-quote.

## Imagery direction

Warm, cinematic, natural light. Hand-felted and wooden textures, real children's
hands mid-play, soft garden or home bokeh, wood surfaces. 4:3 / 4:5 ratios for
editorial; clean studio/flatlay for product-only shots. AI-generated lifestyle
imagery may be used for editorial/atmosphere only — never as a stand-in for a
real product on a PDP.

## Reusable phrases

- "Toys meant to be kept, not outgrown."
- "Scales across a childhood, and across a sibling."
- "Curated by hand. Packed by hand."
- "Built to live together, and to live a long time."
