Materials and construction

The base is pure New Zealand wool, hand needle-felted. The garden florals, stream, and moon-side blossoms are stitched into the base with a second layer of wool to give the surface dimension. The three felted stones are removable and lightly weighted with a wool core. No glues, no polyester backing.

All yarn is OEKO-TEX certified, meaning no harmful dyes or finishes. Production is hand-felted in Nepal in a fair-trade cooperative, where women artisans hand-felt each mat from start to finish.

Ways to play

Small-world storytelling. Place wooden animals on the mat and a story begins on its own.

Day-and-night play. Flip the mat between sides to mark the rhythm of a long afternoon.

Quiet sensory exploration. Younger children spend long minutes tracing the felted stream with a finger, or arranging the three stones into patterns along the garden path.

Co-play. The Large size is built for two children to share without crowding.

Skills it builds

Tactile discrimination across three distinct textures. Narrative imagination and sequencing through two-sided design. Cooperative play and turn-taking. Seasonal and natural vocabulary: stream, blossom, moon, stone, meadow. Unhurried attention, the rarest skill of all to build.

Reggio, Montessori, and Waldorf-aligned homes. Homeschool families building unit studies on seasons, day and night, or nature observation. Occupational therapists for sensory work; preschool teachers as a quiet-corner anchor. Grandparents looking for an heirloom gift that passes to a younger sibling intact.

Care and storage

Spot clean only. Dampen a cloth with cool water and a small amount of wool-safe soap, and rub gently along the surface. Do not submerge or machine wash; the wool will felt further and tighten the textures.

Air dry flat, away from direct sunlight. Like all natural-dye wool, prolonged sun exposure will fade the colors over time. Store rolled in a cotton bag with a cedar block or a few drops of lavender oil.

FAQ

Is it really reversible?

Yes. Both sides are fully designed, hand-felted, and finished. The garden side is spring-green with florals and a stream; the moon side is grey-blue with a white full moon and white-and-grey blossoms.

Are the stones safe for a toddler?

The three felted stones are removable. We recommend supervision for children under three. The florals and stream are stitched to the base and do not detach.

Is it gift-appropriate?

Yes. Every order from our Los Angeles warehouse ships in cream tissue with a handwritten note. Tell us at checkout that it's a gift and we'll leave the price off the packing slip.

Will it fit in the Gus + Mabel Busy Bee Tray?

No. The Large is designed for floor or low-table play, not for the Busy Bee Tray. If you want a smaller felt habitat that fits the Tray, see the Gus + Mabel Coralwhim Cove or Tiny Terrains.
