Quietly open, to the right thing.

We are a small shop with a small bandwidth. When the right collaboration comes along, we move slowly and on purpose. Tell us what you have in mind.

Who we'd love to hear from.

We're not soliciting partnerships, but a thoughtful note from any of the following might find a real welcome.

Makers & studios

Wholesale & new brands.

Small studios making wooden, felt, or natural-material toys with a story we can tell. We curate slowly. We carry brands we love.

Photographers & stylists

Lifestyle photography.

We're always looking for warm, natural-light photography that fits our cream-and-ink visual world. Send us your portfolio.

Educators & therapists

Curriculum partnerships.

Reggio, Montessori, Waldorf, Charlotte Mason. If you write or teach in this world, we'd love to feature your work.

Writers & editors

Editorial features.

Press, podcasts, parenting newsletters, design publications. We are happy to send samples and answer real questions.

How to reach us.

Email is best. Tell us who you are, what you're proposing, and what we'd be doing together. Include any relevant links, brand decks, or sample work.

We read every message. We reply within five business days, usually sooner. If we are not the right fit, we will tell you honestly.

What we are not.

We are not a discount retailer, a paid-influencer marketplace, or a brand who sends free product for posts. We are a small, slow, curated shop, and we choose partnerships on craft and fit, not reach.

If your proposal is a paid-collab-for-followers pitch, you'll save us both time by not sending it.

If you're shopping for a school or a therapist.

You're in the right neighborhood. Our Educator Program offers volume pricing, Net-30 invoicing, and one-on-one curation. Apply there directly.
