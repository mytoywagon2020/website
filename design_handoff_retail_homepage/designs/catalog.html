A letter from the founders

The shop, in one document.

Most catalogs are a list of things to buy. This one is meant to read more like a long letter. The toys are here, with prices and the makers named, but so is the thinking behind them — why we picked what we picked, where the wood comes from, who hand-felts the playmat, what age a particular set really lives with.

We are a small family-run shop in Arcadia, California, online since 2020. Our daughters Hafsa and Noor are the reason we do this. We curate slowly. We say no to a lot of brands. The catalog you are reading covers everything we are confident enough to sell as heirloom.

Scroll through it the way you would a magazine. Each chapter ends with the products in that family. If you are an educator, therapist, or homeschool family, sign in for your bulk pricing and Net-30 terms.

From the workshop

Inside Bumbu, Romania.

Every Bumbu piece is hand-carved from maple, basswood, beech, or oak harvested from FSC-certified European forests. The line is hand-painted in small batches with water-based, non-toxic stains and finished with organic oils and beeswax.

We restock Bumbu directly from the workshop, in person when possible. Most of the pieces in this catalog arrived in our LA warehouse within the last two months.

Hand-carved wood, painted in small batches. A line that scales across childhood, and back onto the shelf each season.Irfana, founder
