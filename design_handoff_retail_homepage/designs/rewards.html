01 / How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

Sign up, shop, redeem. We don't make this complicated, and we don't change the rules on you.

i.

Sign up free.

Click the gift widget in the lower-left corner of any page, register, and you'll start with 100 points in your account.

ii.

Shop the toys.

03 / Questions, briefly

The small print, plainly answered.

When do points expire?

After six months of account inactivity. A new order, a redemption, or a login resets the clock.

What's the minimum order for a reward?

$75 after discounts and before tax. California residents see this floor at the same level. The minimum protects the program from being used purely on shipping-only carts.

Can rewards stack with sales?

Yes, on eligible items. Rewards do not stack with other promotional codes or storewide discount events.

Do I lose points if I return a toy?

Points earned on a returned order are removed from your account. If you used points on that order, those points are not returned; they are considered spent.

Are there brands I can't redeem on?

A small set of premium and price-protected brands are excluded. See the list below.

Can I use this with the Educator Program?

Not stackable. Educator accounts already receive volume pricing and Net-30 invoicing. Choose one or the other on each order.

Brands excluded from reward redemption

Rewards may be redeemed on any brand in our shop except:

Drewart Grapat Fagus Atelier des Peupliers Poppie Toys Mader Kreiselmanufaktur Magic Wood Route Package Protection My Toy Wagon E-Gift Cards Sale items

These brands are price-protected by their makers, which means we can't offer additional discounts on them. We do still earn points on these brands at the standard rate, so a Drewart purchase still builds your balance for a future redemption elsewhere.

Other notes: Points expire after six months of inactivity. Minimum order to apply rewards is $75 after discounts and before tax. Used points are not returned if you return a product. Rewards do not stack with other promotional codes. Referrals are single-use. The birthday gift requires a birthday entered at least 30 days in advance and lands the day before. Account required for all earning and redemption activity.
