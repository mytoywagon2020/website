Our Story

A small toy shop, with a big heart.

Founded by Irfana and Rashid in Los Angeles, inspired by their two daughters, Hafsa and Noor. A boutique built around wooden and felt toys, mindful play, and the long childhoods we hope they'll have.

The founders' daughters

Hafsa & Noor, the heartbeat of the shop.

Welcome to My Toy Wagon.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound inspiration: our two beloved daughters, Hafsa and Noor. They are the heartbeat of our story, grounding us and reminding us daily of the importance of playful, imaginative moments.

My Toy Wagon was founded by Irfana, a devoted mama with a passion for history and toys, and Rashid, a dedicated papa with a keen eye for entrepreneurship. The shop is more than a place that sells toys. It's a labor of love that reflects our commitment to fostering joyful, meaningful play experiences for both children and the adults who love them.

Curated, never collected.

We carefully select wooden and felt toys designed to spark creativity and encourage mindful play. Each piece in our collection is chosen with care to ensure it supports imaginative exploration and creates cherished moments between young and old.

"Playtime is a crucial part of childhood, and a way for families to connect and grow together."

At My Toy Wagon, we believe play is sacred ordinary work. Our shop is dedicated to celebrating the magic of it, and we strive to provide a high-touch experience that makes every visit a small occasion.
