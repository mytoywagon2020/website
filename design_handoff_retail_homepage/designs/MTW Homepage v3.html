Featured brand

Hand-carved in Romania, by the makers at Bumbu Toys.

Each piece in the Bumbu line is shaped from maple and basswood (the American name for linden or lime wood), with beech and oak chosen for heavier landscape elements and large trees. All timber is FSC-certified, harvested from responsibly managed European forests.

Pieces are hand-painted in small batches with water-based, non-toxic stains, then finished with organic oils and/or beeswax. Safe for the toddler who insists on mouthing the horse, durable enough for the older sibling who builds it into a story.

Our Story

A small toy shop, with a big heart.

My Toy Wagon began with a simple inspiration: our two daughters, Hafsa and Noor. They are the heartbeat of the shop, the reminders that childhood is fleeting, and that the toys we choose for it matter.

We carefully curate wooden and felt toys that hold up to real play, that scale across ages, and that pass quietly to a younger sibling. Most orders are packed and shipped by hand from our shop in Los Angeles. A few of the larger, heavier pieces ship directly from our partner warehouses, with the same care.
