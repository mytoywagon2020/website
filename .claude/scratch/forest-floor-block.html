My Forest Floor.

Reclaimed rubberwood. 30+ pieces. Lid flips to chalkboard.

An open-ended tinker tray for woodland scenes. The lid lifts off and flips over to become a chalkboard; internal compartments lift out so the tray can be filled with sand. Pebbles, rocks, mushrooms, beetles, a bird's egg, every play session shaped by curiosity. Made of reclaimed rubberwood, FSC-certified.

  • One Tender Leaf tray with lid, lid flips to chalkboard.
  • 6 gray flat pebbles · 4 multi-faceted rocks · 3 cone trees · 8 twigs.
  • 6 little toadstools + 1 large · 10 leaves · 2 acorns.
  • 3 beetles · 4 tunnel pieces · a snail, a moth, a bird's egg.
  • 11.8 × 11.8 × 2.4 in (30 × 30 × 6 cm).
