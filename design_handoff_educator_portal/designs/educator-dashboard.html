How quotes become orders.

A draft is a hold — pricing locked, stock reserved, nothing ships. An order is a commitment — the PO is in, the warehouse picks, the invoice is generated.

Draft (quote) Cancel any time. No cost. Useful if district approval falls through.
Order (PO confirmed) Cancellation after fulfillment is subject to a 15% restocking fee.

We hold quotes as drafts until your PO arrives, then convert to an order. Net-30 customers with a clean payment history can request earlier conversion — the PO follows as documentation. Shipping is added at conversion.
