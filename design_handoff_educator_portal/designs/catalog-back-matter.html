Chapter seven · How it works

From quote, to PO, to delivery.

Most school orders move from first inquiry to confirmed PO in three to five business days. The process is short by design.

01

Educator account

Apply at mytoywagon.com/educator. Once verified, your account sees educator pricing across the catalog and access to the portal for quotes and order history.

1–2 business days
02

Build a quote

Use the in-portal quote builder, or email educators@mytoywagon.com. Every quote confirms current stock on every line, with itemized educator pricing.

24 hours
03

Submit a PO

Reference the quote number on your PO and email to accounting@mytoywagon.com. We confirm receipt within one business day, with PO acknowledgment.

Same day
04

Fulfillment

Pick-and-pack begins within two business days. Most orders ship within five days; large orders in two to three weeks. Invoice issued at shipment.

5–15 business days
Chapter eight · Procurement notes

The fine print, plainly stated.

Answers to the questions schools tend to ask after a quote has been issued and before a purchase order has been signed.

Stock confirmed on every quote.

Every quote we send confirms current inventory on every line item. If anything is short, we flag it before you submit a PO.

Pricing held for 30 days.

Quoted pricing is honored for 30 calendar days from quote date. Plenty of time for district approval and PO issuance.

If something goes out of stock.

If a quoted item becomes unavailable between quote and PO, we will substitute with your written approval or remove it from the order.

Vendor forms, one business day.

Send your district's vendor onboarding form to accounting@mytoywagon.com and we will return it completed within one business day.

Ship to multiple classrooms.

Drop-ship to multiple classroom addresses on one PO. We do this regularly. Email educators@mytoywagon.com for a coordinated quote.

Sole-source letters, on request.

For districts that require a non-competitive-bid justification (Tara Treasures, Bauspiel, and other genuinely unique brands), we write the letter within two business days.

Tax-exempt, all 50 states.

We accept tax-exempt certificates from any state. Once on file, sales tax is waived on every future invoice automatically.

Sustainability, on the record.

Tara Treasures is Fair Trade USA Certified. Bauspiel and Drewart use FSC-certified European hardwood. Most of our line is OEKO-TEX or GOTS certified.

Cancellations after fulfillment.

PO cancelled after pick-and-pack has begun is subject to a 15% restocking fee plus return shipping. Cancellations before fulfillment are free.

Payment terms.

Net-30 is our default. Net-60 available for larger or recurring orders. Net-60 is our maximum; extensions are reviewed case by case based on need.

References from currently-served schools available on request.
